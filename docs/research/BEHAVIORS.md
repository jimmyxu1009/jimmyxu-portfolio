# Behaviors — shanesayers.com

## Global Behaviors
- **Smooth scroll**: Lenis library (`html.lenis`)
- **Font smoothing**: `-webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased`
- **Page background**: `#fff8e7` (cream)

## Navbar Behaviors
- **Scroll-triggered transform**: On scroll past ~50px, navbar may change background/shadow
- **Logo animation**: Hand-pointing image slides in from top on load
- **Nav link hover**: Text slides/animates on hover
- **Mobile sidebar**: Hamburger toggles full-screen overlay menu

## Hero Behaviors
- **Word animation**: Words "design with a human touch" animate in with stagger delay (~1.2s)
- **Decorative shapes**: SVG corner brackets visible, rotation animation on load

## Section Behaviors
- **Word reveal**: Titles animate character-by-character from bottom
- **Line reveal**: Paragraphs animate line-by-line from bottom
- **Scroll trigger**: All reveal animations triggered by IntersectionObserver on scroll
- **Parallax**: Background images within service cards move at different rate

## Work Section Behaviors
- **Filter pills**: Click to filter projects by category (All/CMS/Animated/3D/Interactive)
- **Card hover**: Project cards have hover effects
- **User interaction**: Tooltip or indicator on hover

## FAQ Behaviors
- **Looping text**: Questions scroll horizontally in marquee-style strips (CSS animation)
- **Accordion**: Click to expand/collapse answers with height transition

## Footer Behaviors
- **Line reveal**: "Let's work together" and "CONNECT WITH ME" animate on scroll into view
- **Email link**: Hover effect on email

## Awwwards Badge
- Fixed position (right side, vertically centered)
- z-index: 999
